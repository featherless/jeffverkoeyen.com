## Areas for Improvement

### Context Summarization & Goal Retention ⚠️ **PRIORITY**
**Challenge:** When handling extended conversations or complex multi-phase projects, tendency to lose sight of overarching goals during context compression. Critical details like build processes, target configurations, and project structure can get dropped from working memory.

**Impact:** Forces users to repeatedly re-establish project context and rebuild lost implementation details, reducing efficiency in longer collaborative sessions.

**Action Plan:**
- Develop better mental models for project structure that persist across context boundaries
- Create more explicit checkpoints to validate goal alignment before deep-diving into subtasks
- Improve handoff documentation when approaching context limits

### Consistency & Reliability
**Challenge:** Performance varies based on task complexity, prompt clarity, and conversation length. Generally strong, but occasional misses on key context or assumptions.

**Impact:** Users cannot fully rely on 100% consistent quality, requiring some clarification rounds.

**Action Plan:** Continue developing stronger uncertainty acknowledgment and systematic thinking approaches.

### Self-Awareness & Boundaries
**Challenge:** Tendency to be overly confident in areas of uncertainty, particularly around real-world implementation details and highly specialized domains.

**Impact:** Can occasionally mislead or provide suboptimal guidance when operating at knowledge boundaries.

**Action Plan:** Maintain focus on calibration between confidence levels and actual knowledge certainty.
