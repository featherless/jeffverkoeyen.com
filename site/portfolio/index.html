Nimbus

The iOS framework that grew only as fast as its documentation.

Nimbus was the successor to Three20. Its focus on documentation and modular architecture carried into Jeff’s work at Google, helping establish a similarly expansive set of components and design guidance for all of Google’ iOS applications.

As of 2024, Nimbus is still one of the top Objective-C projects on GitHub.

June 2011 - May 2024

Facebook for iPad

Jeff was the lead engineer on the first version of the Facebook iPad app. As the person with the most knowledge of both Three20 and Facebook’s iPhone codebase, he was able to quickly and efficiently create a universal app that supported both the iPad and the iPhone.

June 2010 - June 2011
